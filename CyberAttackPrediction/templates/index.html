Project Overview

The escalating sophistication of cyber threats poses significant risks to individuals, organizations, and nations. Cybercrime, encompassing activities like hacking and data breaches, has severe economic and societal consequences.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) offers a powerful arsenal of techniques to address these challenges. This project explores the transition from traditional Machine Learning models to advanced Generative AI approaches for predicting and mitigating cyber attacks.
