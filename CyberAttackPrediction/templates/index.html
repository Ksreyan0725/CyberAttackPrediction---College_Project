Project Overview

Nowadays, cyber attacks are becoming smarter and more dangerous for everyone—from regular people to large companies. Problems like hacking and data theft can cause huge financial losses and harm our society.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) provides us with the best tools to fight back. This project shows how we can use smart computer models, moving from traditional Machine Learning to modern Generative AI, to predict and stop cyber attacks before they happen.
